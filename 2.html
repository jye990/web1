ONECARD GAME!이란,

Game Information

자신이 가지고 있는 카드를 버리면서 진행하는 게임. 마직막 한 장의 카드를 먼저 버리는 사람이 이기는 게임

Rule

1. Flip over so that you can see the back of the card deck.

2. Devide cards to participants according to the number of people

3. Only same shapes, numbers of cards can throw away.

4. If player has one card, must say "One Card"

4-1. But if the player doesn't say anything, the player must take a one card

5. K(King) card can more one card, if the same K card or the same card

6. J(Jack/Jump) card can jump the next player.

7. Q(Queen) card can change the turn that throw away player's cards

8. A(Ace) card can give three cards to the next player

9. A(Ace) Spades card can give five cards to the next player

10. Number 2 cards can give two cards to the next player and defend number 2 card or Joker card

11. Number 3 cards can defend attack cards that number 2 cards

12. If player throw away number 7 cards, the player can say card shape that the player want.

12-1. The next player must throw away cards that shape of card that a former player wants
