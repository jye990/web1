POKER GAME!이란,

Game Information

자신이 갖고 있는 카드를 보면서 베팅을 하며, 가장 높은 가치의 카드 조합을 가지고 있으면 이기는 게임이다.

Rule

Set a dealer and devide the card according to kind of game.

Card Association

1.High Card

-Say 'No Pair'

-Not applicable to any combination

-Among player's card, top of cards, so called top in Korea

2.One Pair

-Combination of the same two cards

3.Two Pair

-Two of combination of the same two cards

4.Three of a Kind

-Say 'Trips'

-Combination of the same three cards

5.Staight

-Show five cards by turns

-Royal Straight is A,K,Q,J,10
